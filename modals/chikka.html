Project Overview

Download the app on your Android device so you can take Chikka with you wherever you go. Simply sign up for free using your mobile number to start texting for free.

You can also sign up using Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus or LinkedIn so you don't have to worry about forgetting your password again. And when you're not on mobile, Chikka is also always accessible on the web.

Features

  • More free texts to the Philippines daily
  • Easy sign-in using Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus or LinkedIn
  • Exchange voice messages with fellow Chikka users
  • Sync Chikka with your local Contacts and have them available even on the web
  • Offline Messaging for Smart & Sun subscribers
  • Automatic 30-day Message Archiving that synchronizes messages in all your devices
