Project Overview

You deserve the best. That's why we are making SmartNet even bigger with SafeZone. SafeZone is the free side of the Internet, where users can access a wide range of websites and apps they need anytime, anywhere without paying for mobile data charges.

Users can enjoy free and unlimited connection to all their lifestyle needs such as their favorite social networks (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram), shopping, news, travel, food, sports, and more to come soon. SafeZone also protects users from unwanted charges by only allowing them to access mobile sites and apps powered by SafeZone.
