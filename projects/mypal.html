Project Overview

Philippine Airlines - Asia's first airline - is the national flag carrier of the Republic of the Philippines. PAL is the country's only full-service carrier, setting the standards in airline service for more than seven decades.

myPAL, Philippine Airline's all-new mobile app, allows you to instantly search and book your next flight to as far as North America in the East and to Europe in the West and to various parts of Asia, the Middle East, Oceania and the Philippines using an all-jet fleet.

Key Features

  • Track your flight status and check-in for your domestic and international flights anytime, anywhere!
